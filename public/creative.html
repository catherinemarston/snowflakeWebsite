Snowflake is a location based meet-up app which connects skiers and riders based on ability level and preferences at the mountain! Finding outdoorsy people has never been easier!

Snowflake integrates with your Facebook page so it will be super easy to create a profile. Upload up to 6 of your favorite pictures(definitely one of you doing something adventurous!), tell us your ski or board level, your home mountain, and the area you want improve!

Snowflake version 1.5 will launch mid-November for iOS and Android! With an improved design, easier navigation, additional features, and bug fixes, we are super excited to be the ultimate meet-up app for outdoorsy people!

To make Snowflake even better, we are asking for your feedback on additional sports, the new events feature, and overall design! We want Snowflake to be the best way for awesome, outdoorsy people to connect!
